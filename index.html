    ARENA is an interdisciplinary research project aimed at better understanding how (conceptual-semantic) knowledge is organized at different levels of abstraction – both in the brain as well as in AI models, and both in the form of concrete visual objects as well as in their linguistic representation. A central part of this research endeavor is to understand - How abstract representations are learned. - How these representations are mapped onto decisions in various task contexts. - Whether and how these representations are coded in neural activity elicited during human perception.
